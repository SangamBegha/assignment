Nepal's Rich Heritage: A Journey Through Time

Bhaktapur Durbar Square

Bhaktapur Durbar Square is a historic site that showcases the beauty of medieval architecture and craftsmanship. It is one of the most beautiful heritage sites in Nepal.

Boudhanath Stupa

The Boudhanath Stupa is one of the largest stupas in Nepal and a UNESCO World Heritage Site. It is an important pilgrimage site for Buddhists around the world.

Changu Narayan Temple

The Changu Narayan Temple is one of the oldest Hindu temples in Nepal. It is known for its intricate wood carvings and ancient inscriptions.

Pashupatinath Temple

Pashupatinath Temple is a sacred Hindu temple complex on the banks of the Bagmati River. It is dedicated to Lord Shiva and attracts devotees from around the world.

Sagarmatha National Park

Sagarmatha National Park is home to Mount Everest and is a UNESCO World Heritage Site. It is a natural paradise that offers breathtaking views of the Himalayas.
